About Dr. Rani

Dr. Venkata Naga Rani Bandaru is the Co-Founder of Bluetick, where she leads AI, Cybersecurity and Cloud-first solution design for service-based projects across domains such as healthcare, education, smart environments and enterprise platforms.

With more than 18 years of experience in teaching, research and academic administration, she specializes in Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain, Cybersecurity, Cloud Computing and Internet of Things (IoT), combining research-grade rigor with production-grade delivery.
